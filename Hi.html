The Hub

The Hub is the centre of student life at Coventry University; meet friends, a meal in the food court, shopping at Costcutter convenience store. The Hub holds Coventry University Student Union and Square One which provides excellent entertainment.

Opening Times:
Monday to Sunday 8am - 10pm

The Hub is home to:

  • The Health and Wellbeing Centre
  • The Spirituality and Faith Centre
  • The Lanchester Gallery
  • The Careers and Add+vantage office
  • The Institute of Applied Entrepreneurship
  • Students’ Union Advice Centre
  • Thefutureworks, Coventry University’s own student employment bureau
